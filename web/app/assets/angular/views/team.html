Dr. Kavita Bhargava

Director - Trainer

Dr. Bhargava is an accomplished Psychologist, Clinical Hypnotherapist and Corporate Trainer. Dr. Bhargava has worked with many educational, health and research institutions in various capacities. Along with the professional practice Dr. Bhargava is presently working as the Director of Indian Institute of Mind and Spiritual Sciences (IIMSS). Dr. Bhargava’s qualifications include PhD- Psychology, NLP Practitioner, IMDHA Certified Clinical Hypnotherapist and Certified Competency Mapping Manager. It is significant to emphasize that Dr. Bhargava is a Gold Medalist in Psychology.

Dr. Bhargava is a sought after Trainer in the country who has contributed more than 300 hrs of training to the industry and has trained more than 3500 participants in Behavioral Sciences as well as Clinical Hypnotherapy. Optum (Health Care Business of United Health Group); EKAA (Formerly known as CHII- California Hypnosis Institute of India); International Trainers Federation, Banasthali University, Rajasthan; the famous Paradise Restaurant, Bangalore; Christ University, Bangalore; Tata Institute for Social Sciences (TISS), Mumbai; Office of the District Education Officer, Jaipur; Eternal Hospital, Jaipur; Institute for Rural Management, Jaipur; HPCL Zonal Trainings, Jaipur; Sophia College, Ajmer; Jail Training Institute, Ajmer; Rashtriya Military School, Ajmer; Mayo College, Ajmer and Mayoor School, Ajmer are few of the organizations that have availed Dr. Bhargava’s services. Dr. Bhargava’s training modules include “Self Management through Mind Sciences”, Personality Development through Mind Sciences”, “Time Management through Mind Sciences”, “Depression and Stress Management”, “Career Counseling through Mind Sciences”, “Integrated Clinical Hypnotherapy Level 1”, “Certificate Course in Counseling with Internship”, “Competency Mapping with Internship”.

Dr. Bhargava holds life memberships to a number of professional bodies like International Medical & Dental Hypnotherapy Association (IMDHA), Indian Academy of Applied Psychology (IAAP) and has also authored a book for Mapping Competencies for State Administrative Services, published in Germany.

Ms. Neerja Handa

Director - Trainer

Ms. Handa is an inspiring individual who aspires to change the way one looks and derive positive meaning out of situations, circumstances and problems in life. Ms. Handa worked with the Garment Industry for more than 25 years of life beautifying the physical body of people in India and abroad till she heard an inner voice that told her to move ahead from body to mind and soul.

Ms. Neerja Handa started her journey in Mind Sciences way back in 2009 with California Hypnosis Institute of India (CHII) now known as EKAA. Since then she has been one of the best Clinical Hypnotherapists and the Facilitator for Level 1-4 of Integrated Clinical Hypnotherapy, EKAA. It is noteworthy to state that Ms. Neerja has been awarded ‘Best Hypnotherapy Trainer’ for four consecutive years.

Ms. Neerja is a renowned Hypnotherapist, Past Life Regression Practitioner, Reiki Practitioner and a Trainer by profession and also an expert in Redikall Healing, Tarot Card Reading, Theta-healing, Soul Group Therapies as known as Family Constellation.

To add to her credits Ms. Handa is an Honorary Member with International Trainers Federation and on the advisory boards of ITF School of Mind Sciences, KPsych Services, Alternative Healing Centre and a number of other organisations working for alternative healing modalities across the country.

Ms. Handa is passionate about promoting Spiritual Mentoring through a scientific approach, which is visible through her programmes like Psycho-Socio- Spiritual Awakening: A Holistic Way to Reach Self-Actualisation, Relationship Engineering, Mystic BEATS, Image Consulting for Alternative Healing Modalities, Spiritual Parenting etc.
